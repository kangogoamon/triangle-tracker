Equilateral triangles

An equilateral triangle is a triangle that has all the three sides with the same length.

Isosceles Triangles

An isosceles triangle is a triangle that has (at least) two equal side lengths.

Scalene Triangles

A scalene triangle is a triangle that does'nt have any side lengths equal to the other.

NOTE:The sum of any two sides of the triangle must be greater than the third side.
